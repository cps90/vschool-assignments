ABOUT US

We welcome you to The Home Collective. Enjoy our unique collections of some of the finest furniture and accessories that offer you distinctive sophistication and comfort. We represent the nation's most highly regarded furniture brands, including Baker, Hickory Chair, Henredon, Hickory White, Lillian August, Hancock and Moore, Stanford, Wesley Hall, Lexington, Stanley, and dozens more of specialty suppliers.

For nearly 50 years, The Home Collective has assisted design conscientious clients with fine home furnishings and interior design. Always emphasizing high quality, great values, and the newest in trends, our store offers a wide assortment of the latest and finest furniture and accessories to help you create a home that truly reflects your lifestyle.

We invite you to visit our beautiful, spacious showroom, always changing with new product, and full of design inspiration. We look forward to welcoming you to our "home," Eldredge Interiors.
